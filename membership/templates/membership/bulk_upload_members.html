Features
  • Bulk Import: Upload hundreds of members at once

  • Error Reporting: Clear feedback on failed rows

How to Use

1
Download Template

Click the "Download Excel Template" button to get the formatted Excel file with sample data and instructions.

2
Fill in Your Data

Open the template and replace the sample data with your member information. Required fields: name, phone, and membership_type.

3
Upload File

Drag and drop your file into the upload zone or click "Choose File" to select it from your computer.

4
Review Results

After upload, you'll see a summary of successful uploads and any errors that need attention.

Important Notes
  • Date format must be YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-12-06)
  • Membership type must be either REGULAR or LIFETIME
  • Gender should be M, F, or O
  • Phone numbers are required and should be unique
  • Empty rows will be skipped automatically
  • The system will validate all data before importing
